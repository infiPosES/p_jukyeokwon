Bamboo
Extract

Every season, the green bamboo stretches straight into the sky. Every bit of the bamboo can be used through and through, a true a symbol of health in nature.

Our company has dubbed our bamboo extract “Jukryeok”. Jukryeok is a pure bamboo extract, made carefully from the finest-quality bamboo leaves and shoots for 24 hours with hydrothermal extraction, a method which releases no harmful substances. Most importantly, Jukryeok contains no tar or toxins, but contains various antioxidants such as polyphenol, saponin, vitamins, 17 different amino acids, and minerals.
