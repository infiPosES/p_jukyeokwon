The Significance of Salt as a Gift

Salt is commonly used in Korea as a thank-you gift for events such as weddings,
birthdays, and funerals. People need salt to survive.
This is why giving salt as a gift represents the wish for “one to be as significant as salt and light”
Giving salt as a heartfelt gift will surely express how much you care.

Methods of
ingestion and drinking

    • Add a small amount in coffee and fruit to enhance flavor.

  • Beverage
